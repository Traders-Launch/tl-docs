# About Traders Launch

Empowering retail traders with instant capital.

![Traders Launch Overview](/img/tl-company-overview.png)

## Business Model

Traders Launch has two arms:

### Consumer Arm
- $50 evaluation entry (lowest in industry)
- 3-5% pass rate
- Same-day uncapped payouts
- Payout guarantee (unique to TL)

### Data & Algo Arm
- $4M+ aggregate buying power
- ML-powered trading strategies
- Risk scoring and edge ranking
- Signal generation potential

## The Trader Journey

1. **Pay $50** — Single, simple entry fee
2. **Trade Futures/Crypto** — On your preferred markets
3. **Hit Profit Target** — No time limit
4. **Same-Day Payout** — Withdraw profits instantly
