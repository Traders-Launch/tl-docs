# Getting Started with Traders Launch

This guide will help you understand how Traders Launch evaluations work and how to get started.

## How It Works

1. **Choose Your Account** - Select an evaluation account size ($25K - $150K)
2. **Pass the Evaluation** - Meet the profit target while following the rules
3. **Get Funded** - Trade with our capital and keep up to 90% of profits

## Evaluation Rules

### Profit Target
- Reach the profit target for your account size
- No time limit to reach the target

### Risk Management
- **Max Daily Loss** - Cannot lose more than the daily limit
- **Max Trailing Drawdown** - Account cannot fall below the trailing threshold
- **Position Limits** - Maximum contracts based on account size

### Trading Requirements
- Minimum 5 trading days
- Trade during regular market hours
- No holding positions overnight (for most products)
