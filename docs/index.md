## What is Traders Launch?

Traders Launch provides funded trading evaluations for futures traders. Pass our evaluation and trade with our capital.

### Key Features

- **Multiple Account Sizes** - $25K to $150K evaluations
- **Futures Trading** - Trade CME, CBOT, NYMEX, and COMEX products
- **Real-Time Analytics** - Track your performance with detailed metrics
- **Fast Payouts** - Weekly payout eligibility for funded traders
